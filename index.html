Multivariable Calculus

Although there are quite a few resources to give an extremely intuitive introduction to single variable calculus, I found that learning multivariable calculus relied heavily on the memorization of formulas and blindly following a teacher. The goal of these videos is to make the long and complicated formulas of multivariable calculus seem intuitive.



Green's Theorem, explained visually

Green's Theorem is a difficult concept from multivariable calculus. This video aims to intuitively introduce Green's Theorem, a relation between a double integral and a line integral.

Fractional Calculus

Fractional Calculus is a branch of calculus that deals with derivative and integrals of order other than whole numbers. It deals with things like the half-deritvate or the ith derivative. This video introduces the idea of the Fractional Derivative and goes over an application to solve a diffrerential equation in physics.
